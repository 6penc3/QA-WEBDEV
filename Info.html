About Tiles Industries – The Art of Calm, Crafted with Care

At Tiles Industries, we believe that tranquility isn’t just a luxury—it’s an essential part of well-being. That’s why we’ve dedicated ourselves to crafting soothing, high-quality soaps that transform your daily routine into a moment of pure relaxation. Each of our artisanal blends is thoughtfully designed to ease aches, calm the senses, and nourish the skin, using only the finest natural ingredients sourced with care.

Our Story Tiles Industries was founded on a simple but powerful idea: self-care should be effortless, natural, and deeply soothing. What began as a small, student-run workshop dedicated to crafting herbal remedies has since blossomed into a leading name in luxurious, calming bath essentials. Inspired by the healing properties of nature, our founders spent years perfecting recipes that would not only cleanse but also restore balance to the body and mind.

From the very beginning, we’ve been committed to quality, sustainability, and innovation. Every bar of soap is handcrafted with a meticulous attention to detail, using time-honored techniques combined with modern skincare science. Our signature blends—like the strawberry-infused Aphrodite's Veil and the cooling, refreshing Minthe’s Kiss—are designed to turn your everyday bath into an immersive spa-like experience.
